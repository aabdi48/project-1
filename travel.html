Nairobi, Kenya

Nairobi, Kenya

I was born and raised in Nairobi, Kenya, where I lived for fifteen years. Nairobi is a vibrant and energetic city that blends modern life with strong cultural traditions. Growing up there shaped my identity, discipline, and appreciation for community.

Many of my friends and family still live in Nairobi. Visiting allows me to reconnect with loved ones and stay grounded in my roots. The city is known for its diverse people, national parks, wildlife, and strong sense of culture.

Traveling between the United States and Kenya has helped me become more adaptable and globally aware. Experiencing different environments has strengthened my communication skills and broadened my perspective.

Nairobi will always be an important part of who I am. It represents my foundation, my early memories, and my motivation to continue growing academically and professionally.
